Project overview:
Car|Rental - Our car rental website offers a user-friendly interface to book vehicles quickly and easily. Choose from a wide range of cars to suit your needs, enjoy competitive prices, and benefit from excellent customer service for a seamless rental experience.

## Features
- User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design and easy navigation for booking cars. design for seamless navigation.  
- Online Booking: Convenient online reservation system. 
- Real-Time Availability: Up-to-date information on vehicle availability.
- Search and Filter: Advanced search and filtering options to find the perfect car.
